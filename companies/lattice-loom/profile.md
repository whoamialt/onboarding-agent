# Lattice Loom — Company Profile

## What we do

Lattice Loom builds workforce planning software for healthcare staffing agencies — the firms that place travel nurses, per-diem techs, and allied health workers into hospitals. Our product replaces the spreadsheets and pager-era scheduling tools these agencies currently run on.

**Mission:** Make shift work less broken.

**Why now:** Healthcare staffing in the US is a $25B/year market that runs on scheduling software built in the 2000s. Hospitals are short-staffed, agencies are profitable, and the workers in the middle get paged at 3am because nobody can find a fill. We sit between the agency and the worker.

## Tech stack

- Backend: Python 3.11, Django, Postgres, Celery
- Frontend: React + TypeScript, Tailwind
- Infra: AWS (us-east-1, multi-AZ), Terraform, GitHub Actions
- On-call: PagerDuty. Platform team rotates on-call (1 week per engineer, every 8 weeks). Product and Data are not on-call.
- Tools: Linear (issues), Slack, Notion (docs), Google Workspace, 1Password, Lattice (perf), Gusto (payroll)

## Compensation philosophy

- Cash at 75th percentile of Boston tech market (Radford SaaS, $50M-$200M revenue band)
- Equity above market at the same percentile
- Bands published internally; new hires can request to see the full band before accepting
- Annual comp review every January; band adjustments in line with market data
- No retention bonuses, no spot bonuses, no signing bonuses *except* for senior+ engineering hires (Senior, Staff, Principal) where market expects it

## Values

- **Rigor.** We get the math right. Hospitals depend on it.
- **Candor.** Direct feedback, in the room, the first time. No backchannel.
- **Low ego.** Best idea wins. Job titles don't.

## Key facts that affect onboarding

- The Platform team carries on-call. Any Platform hire must be on-call ready by Day 30 (paired) and on the rotation by Day 60 (solo).
- Healthcare context means HIPAA training is required for any role that touches scheduling data, which is most roles. Compliance gives a 30-day grace period.
- Hybrid is enforced — Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday in the Seaport office. Remote-only is not offered. Relocation support up to $10K for senior+ hires.
- Annual cycle: comp review January, perf review July, all-hands every other Wednesday.
- Office is one floor of a coworking space; no permanent desks. Hot-desking with team neighborhoods.
