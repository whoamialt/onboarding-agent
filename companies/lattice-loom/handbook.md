# Lattice Loom — Employee Handbook (Excerpts)

> This is the working handbook. Last updated April 2026 by Nicole Peterson. Living document. If something here doesn't match what you're seeing in practice, tell Nicole.

## Hybrid policy

We are in-office Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Mondays and Fridays are office-optional — most engineers work from home on those days, most GTM roles come in.

- Core hours in office: 10am–4pm ET on the three core days
- Visitors are welcome any day; tell Ops by EOD prior so we have desk space
- Out-of-state remote weeks are allowed up to 4 per year, with manager approval
- Permanent remote is not offered. Relocation support up to $10K for Senior+ engineering and director+ roles

## Time off

- 20 days PTO per year, accrued monthly, no cap, no rollover (use it)
- 12 paid holidays (US federal + Indigenous Peoples' Day + day after Thanksgiving + winter close)
- Winter close: December 24–January 1, paid, mandatory
- Sick: separate from PTO, no cap, honor system
- Bereavement: 5 days, immediate family
- Parental leave: 16 weeks paid, primary or secondary, no distinction
- Sabbatical: 4 weeks paid at 5-year mark

## Benefits

- Health: BCBS PPO, 100% employee premium covered, 80% dependents
- Dental + vision: Guardian, 100% covered
- 401(k): Guideline, 4% match, immediately vested
- HSA + FSA available
- Commuter benefits: $315/month pre-tax (MBTA pass at office)
- Home office stipend: $1,500 one-time at hire
- L&D budget: $2,000/year per person
- Mental health: Spring Health, 12 sessions free

## Tools and accounts (Day 1 readiness)

Every new hire needs accounts in:
- Google Workspace (email, Drive, Calendar)
- Slack
- 1Password (shared vaults assigned by team)
- GitHub (engineering only — Platform, Product eng, Data eng)
- Linear
- Notion
- Lattice (performance & feedback)
- Gusto (payroll, benefits enrollment)
- Zoom

Engineering also needs:
- AWS SSO (read-only Day 1, write access after security training)
- Terraform Cloud
- PagerDuty (Platform team only)
- Datadog (read-only Day 1)

## On-call (Platform team only)

- Rotation: 1 week, every 8 weeks
- Shadow week before first solo on-call (paired with the previous on-call)
- On-call training is a 4-hour session run by Diane the first Friday of every month
- Compensation: $500/week stipend, plus comp time for any wake-up page

## Compliance training (required)

| Training | Required for | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| HIPAA basics | Anyone touching scheduling data (~80% of company) | First 30 days |
| Anti-harassment | Everyone | First 30 days |
| SOC 2 awareness | Everyone | First 30 days |
| Code security training | Engineering only | First 60 days, before write access to prod |

## Performance and feedback

- Cycle: full review every July, lightweight check-in every January
- Tool: Lattice
- New hires: 30-day check-in (with HR), 60-day check-in (with manager), 90-day formal review
- 360 feedback at the 6-month mark for managers

## Compensation

Bands are published internally in Notion. Any employee can see their own band and one level above. The full band table is shared on request. Adjustments happen once a year in January based on Radford data.

## How we hire and onboard (today)

We hire ~1-2 per month. Nicole runs offer letters out of Gusto. Ops orders laptops via Apple Business Manager. IT (managed externally by a contractor, Jason) provisions accounts the day before start. Managers are responsible for the first-day welcome and Week 1 calendar. There is a 12-bullet onboarding doc in Notion that hasn't been updated since Nicole joined.
